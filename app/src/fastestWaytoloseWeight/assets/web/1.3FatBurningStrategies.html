How to lose weight fast: Strategy #1

The first fat-burning strategy is to make sure that you always consume your daily "maintenance" calorie intake. This is your TDEE (total daily energy expenditure) or the amount of calories that your body can realistically burn on a daily basis.

This is the fuel that your organism needs to function properly. If you don't provide your body with these calories -- it will always be "only a matter of time" until your body starts to increase your appetite in order to force you to eat more food!

On the other hand -- if you overeat and consume more calories than your body can burn during the day -- then everything is much simpler!

As you see -- your only realistic option is to consume the amount of calories that your body requires on a daily basis to sustain itself! This is the first important step that you must take if you want to create permanent fat loss in the near future!

How to lose weight fast: Strategy #2

The second fat-burning strategy is to make sure that you always use the optimum nutrient ratio, meal frequency, and meal timing approaches for your body type! Most people that have a slow metabolism -- as a rule were born with an endomorph-dominant body type.

Nutrient ratios are simply the percentage of the calories that come from the carbohydrates, proteins, and dietary fats that you consume during the day. Meal frequency is the amount of meals that you consume during the day and meal timing is time of the day when you consume these meals.

If you were born with an endomorph-dominant body type and have a slow metabolism -- the best nutrient ratio to start with is 50-30-20, and the best meal frequency/timing approach is to eat an average sized meal every four walking hours after waking up.
