How To Use This App


Welcome!

This app contains a superb, easy to use CV Maker as well as valuable information about writing your CV. You are welcome to use as much or as little of it as you like.

It's a good idea to go through the information about CVs, first. This gives you valuable help about how to write your CV. The information is below the CV Maker button.  You can go straight to the CV Maker, if you wish.

Click on the menu item called CV Maker to start creating your CV. It is simple to use. Remember, you can download your CV to your phone or tablet or you can email it to yourself or anyone else.  The CV should save to your Download folder. Files can sometimes be difficult to find on mobile devices so just email it to yourself if you cannot find it after downloading.

The CV will save as a Word file (.doc) so that you can use your desktop computer to make it even better, if you wish. Remember to save it as a PDF when you have finished editing it on your desktop to send to employers.
 
There are a selection of CV Templates in this app for you to use. We will be adding more - so please keep this app on your device and check back for updates.

This app is is in Beta mode which means we have completely redeveloped it and you are one of the first people to use it. Please send us your feedback if you get any problems so we can fix things. We'd love to hear from you :) There's a Contact Developer page lower down the app.

Found the app was good? Great! Please write a review on Google Play. We'd really appreciate it.

Good luck with your job hunting.
