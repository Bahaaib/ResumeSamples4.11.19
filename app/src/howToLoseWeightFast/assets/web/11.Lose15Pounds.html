Want to Lose 15 Pounds Fast?

Author: James Jones

It is common that lot of people want to lose fat fast. Today's society likes things to happen fast and weight loss is no different. It goes a long way to understanding why we have fallen into the fast food trap and put on some extra weight which we now need to lose.

The bad part is that while weight goes on fast, it comes off slowly. But if you know some inside secrets there ways to get your body to lose fat quickly and you can lose 15 pounds fast if you need to.

When you want to lose 15 pounds fast you need to make sure that is your single most important goal. You need to take drastic action to lose 15 pounds. Losing 15 pounds fast requires that you stick to a diet and exercise plan. Stick to the program and losing 15 pounds fast is definitely within reach.

The Right Foods to Eat

Let me be very clear about one point. To lose 15 pounds fast you need to implement a diet plan that in one way or another cuts some of calories, including cutting back on your favorite fast foods and drinks.

Cutting back on these things isnt easy so you need to make sure that over the time you are dieting you are 100% committed to losing those 15 pounds otherwise it just wont work for you. To lose weight fast and meet the goals you have set, you dont need to starve yourself of food and spend each day feeling famished, deprived and tired. There are many foods that will give your body energy to keep active and keep up your heart rate.

If you want to lose 15 pounds fast here's a tip.

Put fruits and vegetables on your plate instead of carbohydrates likes rice, bread and pasta. No Im not saying you should become a vegetarian but if you want to lose 15 pounds fast then this is a proven method. And just a reminder that you must eat protein with each meal that you eat. It's a little different between women and men, but as a guideline women should be aiming for 3 ounces of protein sources from lean skinless chicken, fish or lean meat and men aim for 4 to 5 ounces of the same with every meal.

But dont forget protein isnt only in meats. Protein can also be found in foods such as cottage cheese.

Trying to lose 15 pounds fast means that you have to stick to the program. Unlike trying to lose weight in a more natural way you can't take any shortcuts. Put simply if you want to lose 15 pounds fast you have to make sure you follow your plan down to every letter.

So that means you should be cutting out all alcohol, sodas, foods high in sugar and fats, fast food, and cut back on breads, pastas and rice. The best thing that comes out of cutting back on these things is that some people end up changing their diets permanently and they never go back to those high fat and high sugar foods, meaning they are healthier and can manage their weight better.

Heres a tip on sugary treats.

Leave them completely out of your diet and don't tempt yourself by buying a treat for your 'partner' or child and then end up eating it yourself. Sugary foods can destroy your weight loss regime even in small quantities.

You HAVE to Exercise

Okay I now exercise isnt popular but if you want to lose 15 pounds fast you need to move. A good exercise routine should involve you getting moving 3 to 4 times a week.
And because you are aiming for a high weight loss in a short time span, the exercise that you do is very important. Cross training your whole body works the best, the yoga can wait for another time!.

A lot of people are worried when they here the words cross-training but don't let it get to you. People who do cross training find that it's the best way to lose fat quickly and is more effective than the usual gym routines.

Here a quick tip. When your next at the gym don't do the usual and just set the treadmill at your usual running speed. Spend some time on the rowing machine or stationary bike as well.

Give this a go. 10 minutes at your normal pace on the treadmill, 25 minutes on the stationary bike, and 15 minutes on the rowing machine. If you're really up the challenge try the stair climber which is a regular favourite on shows like the Biggest Loser and a proven fat loss exercise.

Through some simple changes to your normal routine you make a big differennce in trying to lose 15 pounds fast. By putting your body through different routines it will get shocked into losing fat.

A proven formula for losing 15 pounds quickly is a mix of cross training and a healthy diet.
