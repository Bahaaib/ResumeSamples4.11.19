Dash Diet

Introduction

This app has been carefully put together to bring you some of the best information about the Dash Diet.  It starts off by telling you all about the diet and then the app goes on to give you some general healthy eating information.  There is also a free book at the end of the app that gives you some healthy juice recipes to get you having more fruit in your diet.

There are also some useful calculators and trackers that you will find as you go through the app.

Please note that due to technical constraints you will find one or two blank pages in the initial documents.  Just scroll on to the next page to keep reading.

Enjoy the app and good luck with your healthy lifestyle!
