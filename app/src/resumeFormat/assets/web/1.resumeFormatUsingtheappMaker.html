Resume Format

Using This App

Welcome!

This app contains a superb, easy to use Resume Builder as well as valuable information about writing your Resume. You are welcome to use as much or as little of it as you like.

Go through the information about Resumes, first. This gives you valuable help about how to write your Resume.

Click on the menu item called Resume Builder to start creating your Resume. It is simple to use. Remember, you can download your Resume to your phone or tablet or you can email it to yourself or anyone else.  The Resume should save to your Download folder. Files can sometimes be difficult to find on mobile devices so just email it to yourself if you cannot find it after downloading.

The Resume will save as a Word file (.doc) called resume.docx so that you can use your desktop computer to make it even better, if you wish. Remember to save it as a PDF when you have finished editing it on your desktop to send to employers.

There are a selection of Resume Templates in this app for you to use. We will be adding more - so please keep this app on your device and check back for updates.

This app is is in Beta mode which means we have completely redeveloped it and you are one of the first people to use it. Please send us your feedback if you get any problems so we can fix things. We'd love to hear from you :) There's a Contact Developer page lower down the app.

Found the app was good? Great! Please write a review on Google Play. We'd really appreciate it.

Good luck with your job hunting.
