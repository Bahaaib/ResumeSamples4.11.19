Cover Letter

How To Use This App

Welcome!

This app contains a superb, easy to use Cover Letter Builder as well as valuable information about writing your Cover Letter. You are welcome to use as much or as little of it as you like.

Go through the information about Cover Letters, first. This gives you valuable help about how to write your Cover Letter.

Click on the menu item called Cover Letter Builder to start creating your Cover Letter. It is simple to use. Remember, you can download the finished letter to your device or you can email it to yourself or anyone else.  The Cover Letter should save to your Download folder. Files can sometimes be difficult to find on mobile devices so just email it to yourself if you cannot find it after downloading.

The Cover Letter will save as a Word file (.docx) so that you can use your desktop computer or mobile device to make it even better, if you wish. Remember to save it as a PDF when you have finished editing it to send to employers.

There is a selection of Cover Letter Templates in this app for you to use. We will be adding more - so please keep this app on your device and check back for updates.

BONUS - You can now download the Cover Letter Templates used in the Cover Letter Builder as blank documents for free. See the app's Home Screen for details.

This app is in Beta mode which means we have completely redeveloped it and you are one of the first people to use it. Please send us your feedback if you get any problems so we can fix things. We'd love to hear from you :) There's a Contact Developer page lower down the app.
